Story of the Founders

Hey, we are Matthew and Sara!
We met at the University of Exeter, where Matthew was studying Engineering and Sara was specialising in Fine Art. We've been together ever since, and are now joined by our dog, Toby (who you may have noticed features in some of our product photographs).

Design Choices

Incorporating comfort and luxury into our products was our number one priority.
We have always been passionate about making our house a home, and since the lockdown have found ourselves, like many families, trying to find the perfect pieces to make our home work exactly as we would like it to. That is why we decided to create SnuggleZone, so that we could combine our knowledge of aesthetic beauty and engineering to innovate furniture that looks inviting and is unbeatably comfortable, so that you have furniture which will invite you to relax and bring serenity into the heart of your home.
As our business is still growing, we are currently unfortunately only able to offer delivery within the United Kingdom. However, it is our ambition that with time SnuggleZone will ship globally and offer our simple comfort to the world!

Sustainability

It is extremely important to us that our products incorporate the environment at every level. This is why all of our sofas use 50% recylced stuffing and are covered with 100% natural or recycled. materials. The only plastic that is used in our range is recycled. Also, all of our products come with a 10-year warranty, which will ensure that this furniture is made to last.
